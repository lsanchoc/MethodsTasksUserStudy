The Excel book "ExpertStudy-Participants answers" contains the following sheets:

1. Participants: information on participants.
2. Answ-effectiveness: answers given by participants in the order in which they answered.  
3. Answ--ordered-by-method: answers given by participants ordered by method.
4. Answ-effectiveness-frequency: count of correct answers per exercise per method.
5. Answ-correctness %: percentage of correct answers per exercise per method.
6. Answ-qualitative and codes: participants' outloud comments and feedback for each question and the codes that we assigned.
7. Ratings Likert & feedback: Likert scale ratings given by participants as well as their feedback, and some basic statistics that we calculated.
8. Ratings Likert summary: a summary of the Likert scale ratings.
